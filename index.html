About the Workshop

Recent advances in Large Language Models (LLMs) have created new opportunities for robot learning and planning. LLMs provide powerful semantic reasoning, task decomposition, instruction understanding, and commonsense knowledge, while human feedback and demonstrations can improve alignment, safety, and adaptability in real-world robotic systems.

This workshop focuses on the integration of LLMs, human guidance, and robotic learning/planning methods. We aim to bring together researchers working on embodied AI, human-in-the-loop learning, interactive robot planning, and human-robot collaboration to discuss how robots can learn, reason, and act with both model-based intelligence and human support.
