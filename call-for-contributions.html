Call for Contributions

Contribute to the Workshop

Overview

We invite researchers and practitioners to submit contributions related to LLM-augmented robot learning, human-guided robot planning, embodied AI, and interactive human-robot systems. The workshop welcomes both theoretical and applied work, including early-stage ideas, system demonstrations, benchmark studies, and position papers.

Topics of Interest

  • Large language models for robot planning and reasoning
  • Human-in-the-loop reinforcement learning and imitation learning
  • Learning from human feedback, correction, and preference
  • Task and motion planning with language-based priors
  • Multimodal grounding for embodied agents
  • Interactive policy learning and shared autonomy
  • Safe and trustworthy LLM-based robot decision-making
  • Evaluation protocols for human-LLM-robot systems
  • Applications in service robots, social robots, domestic robots, assistive robotics, and industrial robotics
